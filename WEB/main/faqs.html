Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What is ReStrat about?
A: ReStrat is a community to help people in need, after a natural disaster.

Q: Should I pay a membership?
A: No, ReStrat does not require a paid membership.
You can join us for free!

Q: How can I join ReStrat?
A: All you have to do is to complete your name and your personal infos at the homepage. After that our administration team will review your request and they will send you your credentials.

Q: How can I ask for help?
A: You can sign up for free and our volunteers will provide you with the best available goods.

Q: Can I volunteer remotely?
A: Yes! We are searching for new members to our developing and to our administration teams.
